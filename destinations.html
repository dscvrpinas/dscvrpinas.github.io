DESTINATIONS

LUZON

The largest and most popular island in the Philippines and it's known for its mountains, beaches and coral reefs.

  • Mt. Daraitan
  • Kwebang Lampas
  • Burot Beach
  • Tambobong Beach
  • Talisayin Cove

VISAYAS

The smallest of the three main divisions in the Philippines. Visayas offers the tourists its rich culture, white sand beaches, grass-covered mounds that turn brown in the dry season and many more places to be discovered.

  • Apo Island
  • Gigantes Island
  • Mt. Kanlaon
  • Sumilon Island
  • Marabut Samar

MINDANAO

The second largest and southernmost major island in the Philippines. is considered the food basket of the Philippines and eight of the top 10 agri-commodities exported from the Philippines

  • Tinago falls
  • Enchanted River
  • Britania Islands
  • Dahilayan Forest Park
  • Pearl Farm Beach Resort
